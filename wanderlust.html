Wanderlust
— Brand & App UI

Logo, identity, and a full travel booking app UI from scratch — built for a real client outside the lighting sector.

Overview

Brand and product from a single blank canvas

Wanderlust Travel by Dawn is a travel brand built from scratch — logo, identity, and a full app UI covering every stage of the user journey. The brief was to create a brand that felt human, aspirational, and distinct within a travel category full of generic visual languages.

The app scope was comprehensive: onboarding, login and account creation, location setup, search and discovery across Berlin, Barcelona, Lisbon, and Rome, listing views, booking and cart, checkout with payment (Bank, COD, EMI, MFS, Card), slide-to-pay interaction, payment success and failure states, order tracking, and full account management.

Challenge vs. Response
Challenge

Creating a travel brand that feels genuinely distinct in a category dominated by generic visual clichés and sea of similar logos.

Response

Developed a logo mark that merges a plane, a bird, and a subtle reference to the Airbnb symbol into one fluid hand-drawn form — signalling a brand that cares about the journey, not just the destination.

Challenge

Designing 20+ app screens that feel instinctive and premium, while covering a complex flow from search to payment to tracking.

Response

Mapped the full user architecture before designing a single screen. Built a consistent UI language across every state, with clear information hierarchy and a restrained colour palette chosen for flexibility at every scale.

Challenge

Making a brand identity and an app UI feel like one coherent product, not two separate outputs bolted together.

Response

Developed the brand system and the UI system in parallel — so the type, colour, and tone established in the identity fed directly into every screen, creating a product that felt genuinely brand-led rather than template-driven.

The mark had to carry a whole brand promise — travel as experience, not transaction. Everything else followed from that.

— Wanderlust Travel by Dawn, 2025
Process

From concept to brand to product

01
Brand concept + positioning
Started with the brand’s core idea: travel as experience, not transaction. Defined the visual and tonal territory — fluid, personal, aspirational, human — before any mark-making began.
02
Logo mark development
Explored multiple directions before arriving at the final mark — a form that merges a plane, a bird, and a subtle nod to the Airbnb symbol into a single fluid shape. The hand-drawn quality was intentional: it signals a brand that cares about the journey.
03
Brand identity + application testing
Tested the logo mark across digital interfaces and brand assets. Developed the wider identity system — type, colour, and tone — that would carry through into the app design.
04
App UI — architecture + flow
Mapped the full user journey before designing any screens: onboarding, login, location setup, search and discovery, listing views, booking and cart, checkout, payment, order tracking, and account management.
05
UI screen design
Designed the complete screen set — 20+ screens from welcome through to payment success, order tracking, and full account management. Every screen brand-consistent, every flow tested for clarity.
