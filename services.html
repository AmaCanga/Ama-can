How I work

01

Brief

Tell me what you're shipping, when it needs to be done, and what good looks like to you.

02

Structure

Before anything gets designed, I map the structure - what needs to exist, how it fits together, what the system needs to do.

03

Build

Design and template in stages. You see it at key points - not just at the end when it's too late to change direction.

04

Handoff

Files, templates, and enough documentation that your team can keep it going without coming back to me every time.
