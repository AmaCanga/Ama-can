About

I solve the structure problem first.

Structure first, then aesthetics. I've been doing this for 12 years and that order has never changed. Get the hierarchy right, remove anything that isn't earning its place, then make it look good.

Background

How I work

I specialise in brand and product design for complex catalogues and marketplace environments. After 60+ projects across packaging, e-commerce, and brand systems, I've learned that most design problems are structure problems first.

The briefs I work best on have a real operational problem underneath them - a range that's grown too fast, a catalogue that takes 9 months to produce, a brand that's been ignored for years. That's where I start.

Based in Lostock, Bolton. Work with brands across the UK and Europe - directly, or embedded within their team.

How I think

Design principles I work by

01
Structure before style
Hierarchy, naming, system logic. That work happens before any aesthetic decisions.
02
Build it so others can use it
If the client needs me every time they want to update it, I haven't finished the job. Templates should run without me.
03
Clarity over decoration
Premium doesn't mean complicated. The best version is usually the one with less in it.
04
Fast doesn't mean sloppy
Speed comes from having a proper system set up at the start. Cut corners on that and you pay for it later.
